Safety Measures Digital Commerce

When it affects infrastructures or essential services such as electricity networks, telecommunications, transport systems, banking services or major government sites, a cyber attack causes disruptions that can greatly jeopardize services for the public, and have very serious economic consequences. A cyber attack is a malicious act by persons, groups or organizations against a computer system that is intended to destroy or damage it, fool it or take control of it in order to carry out illegitimate operations or steal data. A cyber attack can be against a computer, a server, peripherals or mobile communication devices . Such an act is usually committed through a local network or an Internet connection.
